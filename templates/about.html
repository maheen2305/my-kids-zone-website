About Our School

My Kids Zone Pre-Primary School was established in 2019 under the leadership of Megha Vora, an experienced educator with over 12 years of expertise in early childhood education.

Our mission is to create a nurturing, joyful, and stimulating environment where every child feels valued, safe, and inspired to learn.

We focus on holistic development — nurturing academic, social, emotional, and physical growth.

“Every child is unique and deserves an education that celebrates their individuality while building a strong foundation for lifelong learning.” – Megha Vora, Founder & Director
